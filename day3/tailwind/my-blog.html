Waking Up in the Hills

Sometimes, all it takes is a change of view to reset your soul. Last weekend, I left behind the buzzing streets and blinking screens for something simpler — the hills of Manali. I didn’t have a big plan. Just packed a small bag, booked a sleeper bus, and let the journey take care of itself.

Momos, Bonfire & Laughter

In the evening, I joined a few backpackers by a small bonfire in the hostel courtyard. Someone played a guitar, someone served hot momos, and everyone shared stories like old friends — even though we’d just met. That’s the magic of the mountains.
